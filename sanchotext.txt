What is Cardano?

Cardano is a blockchain platform for changemakers, innovators, and visionaries, with the tools and technologies required to create the possibility to bring about positive global change.

Cardano is a proof-of-stake blockchain platform with a consensus mechnism call Ouroboros: the first to be founded on peer-reviewed research and developed through evidence-based methods. It combines pioneering technologies to provide unparalleled security and sustainability to decentralized applications.

With a leading team of engineers, Cardano can redistribute power from unaccountable structures to individuals. Giving cenntralized power back to people.
